About Us

We as Tsar-Dev-Collective are a small international
group of passionate artists based near New Orleans, LA, determined to share our love of digital media through our software.
We are committed to creating both enjoyable games for our audience and thoughtful software design for our partners.

Alongside our friends at Nihnyx hardware, it is ultimately our dream to re-live the days when "fun" didn't have to be about money.
As such, we here at Tsar-Dev will always pride ourselves on prioritizing good design and engaging gameplay above all else,
and on keeping everything we produce at a reasonable cost just to ensure we can keep the lights on.

Your time is invaluable to us, and every moment you spend with one of our programs should be special.
So, we thank you for giving us the time to read our mission, and we hope you can enjoy what we have in store for you.
